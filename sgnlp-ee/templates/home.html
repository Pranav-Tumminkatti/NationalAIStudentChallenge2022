Why Make This?

CyberAbuse Detector

Nearly half of the 1,000 respondents - Singaporeans and permanent residents aged above 15 - had experienced some form of online harm such as being stalked online and cyberbullied.About 43 per cent of them said they would not take action because they believe it would not make a difference. Hence, our team hopes to make Singapore a more cyber-secure space, by sieving out instances of cyberbullying online. Usually, these cases of cyberbullying need to be reported by users in order to come to the attention of authorities. However, our team would like to automatically detect instances of cyberbullying and alert the social media moderators.

Plagarism Checker

Plagarism negatively impacts the original creators of various published works and is an unfair use of other's material. Our plagarism checker ensures that any writings (eg. blogs or articles) published on the internet is free of plagiarism. This is done by cross checking with other similar writeups on the internet, and providing a threshold as to how much the contents can be similar to one another before reporting cases of plagiarism.
